Abstrction

One of the most important tasks in the research process is to review the relevant literature. It is a compilation of previous study findings on connected issues that are directly and indirectly related. This review informs researchers on what is already known and what needs to be tested in relation to the research issue. It assists the researcher in avoiding duplication of effort and offers helpful suggestions for additional research on the subject. A literature review is an examination of scholarly sources on a particular subject. It gives us a broad picture of current knowledge, helping us to spot pertinent ideas, methodologies, and research gaps. In general, the goal of a review is to critically examine a portion of a published body of information by summarising, classifying, and comparing previous research investigations of literature and theoretical pieces.
